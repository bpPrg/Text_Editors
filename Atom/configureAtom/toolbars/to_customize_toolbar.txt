To customize atom toolbar
===========================
1. first install
tool-bar
flex-tool-bar
awesome-tool-bar

Edit toolbar.cson from toolbar icon just created.
open ~/.atom/toolbar.cson

flex-tool-bar does not need to restart Atom.

3. available icons
To find available icons go to:
open ~/.atom/packages/tool-bar/iconsets

we see 6 packages:
devicon, foundation, ionicons, font-awesome, icomoon, mdi

example:
iconicons/_ionicons-variables.less
